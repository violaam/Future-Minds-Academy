Who is Viola Maliqi?

Meet Viola Maliqi from Gjilan, Kosovo. Viola pursued her studies in Primary Education at the University of Prishtina "Hasan Prishtina" and is currently working towards a master's degree in Science of Education. With a strong desire to inspire and empower young minds, Viola has chosen teaching as her true calling. As a second-grade teacher, Viola constantly seeks innovative teaching methods to create an engaging and interactive learning environment for her students. She believes in the power of technology and incorporates it into her lessons to enhance the educational experience. In fact, Viola's goal is to expand her knowledge in coding so that she can integrate it into her classroom and profession, providing her students with valuable skills for the future. Outside of the classroom, Viola indulges in hobbies that fuel her creativity. She enjoys photography, capturing beautiful moments and expressing her artistic side. Additionally, she loves exploring the great outdoors, finding inspiration in nature's wonders.
